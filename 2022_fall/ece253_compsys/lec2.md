## Number Systems

* Different bases exist:
	* Decimal (base 10)
	* Binary (base 2)
		* Commonly prefixed with $0b$
	* Hexadecimal (base 16)
		* Commonly prefixed with $0x$ or $0h$
		* Each hex digit corresponds to 4 binary digits, allowing a more compact way to write it down
